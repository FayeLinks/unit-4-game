Moria or Less

The Mines of Moria have long overflown with gold, silver, precious gems and iron, that you and your family have unearthed for generations.

Your responsibility as a young Dwarf is to gather as much in your mining cart as possible and bring it back to the Great Forges to the awaiting blacksmiths.

However, the pathway along the mines can be treacherous, and if you overload your cart the haul will collapse the mining bridges!

Your goal is to load your cart to the maximun number of pounds allowed for the mining path you're prospecting without exceeding the limit.
